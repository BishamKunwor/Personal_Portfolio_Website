Inspired Page

While searching for some website, slidebean was encountered where the sign-up page facinated the design aspect of the designer. The designer used shadow to provide a 3d effect of depth of field to the flyer card. This stroke me an idea to create hover effect on the boxes that are used in my site.

My Page

The end goal in this website is to provide hover effect in every box element that is present inside this site. To make the illusion of the 3d hover effect, scaling had to be done. With the scaling, box shadow was introduced to give the box depth of field. The shadow also needed more spread to make the 3d effect illusion.
