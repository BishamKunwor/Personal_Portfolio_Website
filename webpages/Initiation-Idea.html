Inspired Page

While using facebook, the color choice of the designer facinated me. They used only 5 (at most) color to beautifully represt their website. The designers used bright color to indicate something that is important in the webpage while coloring other aspects which are not necessary to user with suttle or light color.

My Page

The concept was used in my page. As my page's background does not need much attention so, soft and light background color was used. Also bright color i.e., dark blue was used in order to highlight most important aspect of the page. This page only used four colors to represent each necessary data it contains.
